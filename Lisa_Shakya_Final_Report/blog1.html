NEPAL

Nepal is a landlocked country in South Asia, located between India and China, and is best known for its rich cultural heritage and stunning natural beauty. It is home to the Himalayas, including Mount Everest, the highest peak in the world, and has a diverse landscape ranging from high mountains to fertile plains. Kathmandu is the capital city and cultural heart of the nation. Nepal is the birthplace of Lord Buddha and is characterized by religious harmony, with Hinduism and Buddhism being the major religions. The country has a diverse population with many ethnic groups, languages, and traditions, and festivals such as Dashain and Tihar play an important role in social life. Tourism, agriculture, and remittances are key parts of Nepal’s economy, making Nepal a unique blend of natural wonder, history, and living tradition.
