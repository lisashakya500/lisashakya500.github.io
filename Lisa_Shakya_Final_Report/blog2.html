EUROPE

Europe is a continent located entirely in the Northern Hemisphere and mostly in the Eastern Hemisphere, known for its long history, cultural diversity, and economic development. It is made up of more than 40 countries and has been the birthplace of many influential civilizations, ideas, and political systems. Europe features a wide variety of landscapes, including mountains, plains, rivers, and coastlines, and is home to famous landmarks such as the Alps, the Eiffel Tower, and the Colosseum. The continent has a rich cultural heritage expressed through art, music, literature, and architecture, and it plays a major role in global politics, science, and trade. Despite its relatively small size, Europe has had a significant impact on world history and continues to be an important center of culture, education, and innovation.
