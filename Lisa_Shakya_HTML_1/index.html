Jurassic Park

Author/Director: Michael Crichton

Genre: Science fiction, Thriller, Adventure, Action

Year Released: 1993

Awards: Academy Awards (Oscars), BAFTA Awards, Saturn Awards, Grammy Award
Fun Fact: The dinosaur roars were created by mixing real animal sounds.

Jurassic Park, is baesd on a wealthy entrepreneur builds a theme park featuring living dinosaurs cloned from ancient DNA. Before opening to the public, he invites a group of scientists and his grandchildren to tour the park. When the park’s security systems fail due to sabotage, the dinosaurs escape, and chaos erupts across the island.
The film explores themes of scientific ethics, humanity’s attempt to control nature, and the unforeseen consequences of technological ambition all while delivering thrilling survival sequences.
